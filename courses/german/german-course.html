Learn German from A1 to C2 levels

What You Will Learn

A1 Level - Beginner

  • Chapter 1: Basic greetings and introductions
  • Chapter 2: Numbers, colors, and common objects
  • Chapter 3: Definite and indefinite articles
  • Chapter 4: Regular verbs and simple sentences

A2 Level - Elementary

  • Chapter 1: Past tense (Perfekt) and time expressions
  • Chapter 2: Daily routines and separable verbs
  • Chapter 3: Modal verbs and sentence structure
  • Chapter 4: Dative case and prepositions

B1 Level - Intermediate

  • Chapter 1: Präteritum (simple past) and narrative
  • Chapter 2: Passive voice and formal communication
  • Chapter 3: Subjunctive mood (Konjunktiv II)
  • Chapter 4: Relative clauses and complex sentences

B2 Level - Upper-Intermediate

  • Chapter 1: Advanced grammar structures
  • Chapter 2: Genitive case and academic writing
  • Chapter 3: Idiomatic expressions and colloquialisms
  • Chapter 4: Reading comprehension and discussion

C1 Level - Advanced

  • Chapter 1: Advanced vocabulary for professional contexts
  • Chapter 2: Complex sentence structures and nuance
  • Chapter 3: German literature and cultural references
  • Chapter 4: Debates and persuasive communication

C2 Level - Proficient

  • Chapter 1: Near-native fluency and sophistication
  • Chapter 2: Specialized vocabulary for academic fields
  • Chapter 3: Stylistic writing and rhetorical devices
  • Chapter 4: Advanced cultural competence and dialect understanding

A2 Elementary - Chapter 1: Everyday Life

In this chapter, you will learn how to talk about your daily routine, family, and use simple past tense.

Key Vocabulary

  • Wake up, get up, have breakfast, go to work/school, come home, have dinner, go to bed
  • Mother, father, sister, brother, parents, children
  • Yesterday, last week, last year

Grammar Focus: Simple Past Tense

The simple past is used for actions that happened in the past. Example: "I visited my friend yesterday."

  • I played football last weekend.
  • She watched a movie yesterday.
  • We went to the park.

Practice

  1. Write five sentences about what you did last weekend.
  2. Describe your family using the new vocabulary.

C2 Proficient - Chapter 1: Idioms and Advanced Topics

In this chapter, you will learn about idiomatic expressions, phrasal verbs, and how to discuss complex topics fluently.

  • Break the ice
  • Hit the books
  • Once in a blue moon
  • To be on the same page

Grammar Focus: Advanced Structures

Use inversion, cleft sentences, and other advanced grammar. Example: "Never have I seen such a beautiful view."

Practice

  1. Use three idioms in a short story.
  2. Discuss a complex topic using advanced grammar.

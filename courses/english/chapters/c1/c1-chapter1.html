C1 Advanced - Chapter 1: Academic English

In this chapter, you will learn how to write academic essays, understand lectures, and use advanced connectors and vocabulary.

  • Firstly, secondly, finally
  • In conclusion, therefore, however
  • According to recent research...
  • It is important to note that...

Grammar Focus: Complex Sentences

Use subordinate clauses to add detail and complexity. Example: "Although it was raining, we went outside."

Practice

  1. Write an introduction for an academic essay.
  2. Listen to a short lecture and summarize the main points.

B1 Intermediate - Chapter 1: Experiences and Opinions

In this chapter, you will learn how to talk about your experiences, express opinions, and use the present perfect tense.

Key Vocabulary

  • Experience, travel, visit, try, enjoy, opinion, agree, disagree
  • Already, yet, ever, never, just

Grammar Focus: Present Perfect

The present perfect is used for experiences and actions that have happened at an unspecified time. Example: "I have visited London."

  • Have you ever eaten sushi?
  • I have just finished my homework.
  • She has never been to Paris.

Practice

  1. Write about an experience you have had using the present perfect.
  2. Express your opinion about a movie or book.

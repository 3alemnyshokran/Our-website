B2 Upper-Intermediate - Chapter 1: Debates and Discussions

In this chapter, you will learn how to participate in debates, express agreement and disagreement, and use advanced vocabulary and modal verbs.

Key Vocabulary

  • Debate, discuss, argument, point of view, agree, disagree, suggest, recommend
  • Should, must, might, could, would

Grammar Focus: Modal Verbs for Opinion

Use modal verbs to express opinions and suggestions. Example: "You should try this method."

  • We must consider all the options.
  • You might be right.
  • Could you explain your point of view?

Practice

  1. Debate a topic with a partner using modal verbs.
  2. Write an essay expressing your opinion on a current issue.

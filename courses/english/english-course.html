All levels from A1 to C2

What You Will Learn

A1 Level - Beginner

  • Chapter 1: Basic pronouns and the verb "to be"
  • Chapter 2: Colors, numbers, and basic vocabulary
  • Chapter 3: Family vocabulary and possessive adjectives
  • Chapter 4: Simple present tense and everyday activities

A2 Level - Elementary

  • Chapter 1: Past tense and time expressions
  • Chapter 2: Daily routines and adverbs of frequency
  • Chapter 3: Shopping vocabulary and comparatives
  • Chapter 4: Giving directions and prepositions of place

B1 Level - Intermediate

  • Chapter 1: Present perfect tense and experiences
  • Chapter 2: Future forms and making plans
  • Chapter 3: Modal verbs and giving advice
  • Chapter 4: Narrative tenses and storytelling

B2 Level - Upper-Intermediate

  • Chapter 1: Conditionals and hypothetical situations
  • Chapter 2: Passive voice and formal language
  • Chapter 3: Reported speech and news reporting
  • Chapter 4: Complex sentence structures

C1 Level - Advanced

  • Chapter 1: Advanced vocabulary and idioms
  • Chapter 2: Complex grammar structures
  • Chapter 3: Persuasive language and debate
  • Chapter 4: Critical analysis and reading

C2 Level - Proficient

  • Chapter 1: Native-like fluency
  • Chapter 2: Professional vocabulary
  • Chapter 3: Sophisticated literary analysis
  • Chapter 4: Advanced rhetoric techniques

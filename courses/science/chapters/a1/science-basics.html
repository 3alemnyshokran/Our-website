Introduction to Science

Science is the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experimentation. It helps us understand the world around us.

In this chapter, we'll learn about:

  • The scientific method
  • Basic scientific concepts
  • Simple experiments you can do at home
  • How science impacts our everyday lives

Science begins with curiosity and a desire to understand how things work.

The Scientific Method

The scientific method is a process used by scientists to study the world around them. It involves these steps:

  1. Ask a question

    Start with something you're curious about.

  2. Research

    Find out what is already known about your question.

  3. Form a hypothesis

    Make a prediction about what might happen.

  4. Test with an experiment

    Design and conduct an experiment to test your hypothesis.

  5. Analyze data

    Look at the results of your experiment.

  6. Draw conclusions

    Decide if your hypothesis was correct.

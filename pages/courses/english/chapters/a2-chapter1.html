A2 - Elementary

Everyday Activities and Daily Routines

At the A2 level, you can understand and talk about daily routines and everyday activities using the present simple tense and frequency adverbs.

Frequency Adverbs

We use these words to say how often we do activities:

  • Always (100% of the time)
  • Usually (80% of the time)
  • Often (70% of the time)
  • Sometimes (50% of the time)
  • Rarely/Seldom (20% of the time)
  • Never (0% of the time)

Key Learning Points:

  1. Using the present simple tense to talk about routines and habits
  2. Placing frequency adverbs correctly in sentences
  3. Asking and answering questions about daily activities
  4. Describing your typical day using appropriate vocabulary

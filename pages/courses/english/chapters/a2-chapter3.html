Describing Places

At the A2 level, you can understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevance, including local geography and your immediate environment.

Home and Accommodation

Vocabulary and phrases to describe where you live:

  • Types of homes: house, apartment/flat, cottage, bungalow, villa
  • Rooms: bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, living room, dining room, study, garage
  • Furniture: bed, table, chair, sofa, armchair, wardrobe, desk, bookshelf
  • Location phrases: "I live in a house/apartment.", "I live in the city center/suburbs.", "My apartment is on the second floor."

Describing Cities and Places

Useful adjectives and phrases to describe places:

  • Size: big, small, huge, tiny
  • Age: new, old, modern, ancient, historical
  • Appearance: beautiful, ugly, clean, dirty, crowded, empty
  • Atmosphere: quiet, noisy, busy, peaceful, lively

Talking About Family

At the A2 level, you can understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevance, including basic personal and family information.

Family Members Vocabulary

Here are important words to describe your family:

  • Immediate family: parents (mother/mom, father/dad), siblings (brother, sister), spouse (husband, wife), children (son, daughter)
  • Extended family: grandparents (grandmother/grandma, grandfather/grandpa), aunt, uncle, cousin, niece, nephew
  • In-laws: mother-in-law, father-in-law, brother-in-law, sister-in-law
